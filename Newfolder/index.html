GEM PARK

At the west of the Greater Cairo Region, a civil focus lights up, which will illuminate a new path for the global human community when one of the most important national projects is ever completed. That project will be an urban space with a degree of a civilized national nucleus that carries with it the genes of the unique Egyptian past and the accumulated chromosomes of Egyptian society next to the first and last wonder of the world “foot of the pyramids” linking it to one of the most important keys of the cultural and civilizational heritage “The Grand Egyptian Museum”.

This vacuum is also called the “GEM PARK”

As Grand Egyptian Museum’s Park, at this genius and unique site on an area of 560,000 square meters with its geographical and spatial surroundings between the museum and the pyramids of Giza, will cause a qualitative shift in the different fields that will mainly contribute to the restoration of Egypt on top the global tourism map again through a group of recreational, cultural and social uses and activities, as we have promised by the political desire that insists on moving forward in order to achieve its wise vision.
