Integrated IT Solutions: Enhancing Connectivity, Security, and Efficiency

In today's fast-paced digital environment, businesses need robust and integrated IT systems to stay competitive, secure, and efficient. Integrated IT solutions bring together multiple technological services under one unified platform, ensuring seamless operations across various business functions. Our range of solutions provides cutting-edge technology and strategic expertise to enhance every aspect of your organizationâ€™s IT infrastructure. At Light Current, we offer comprehensive services that cover all essential areas of IT, including enterprise networking, network security, wireless systems, call center solutions, and advanced CCTV systems. Here is a detailed overview of our offerings:

1. Enterprise Networking Routing: Efficient and reliable data routing for seamless communication across your network.
Switching: Advanced switching solutions to improve connectivity and reduce network congestion.
Redundancy: Ensuring uninterrupted operations with backup systems to handle failures.
Monitoring: Continuous monitoring to detect and resolve issues proactively.
LAN: Local Area Network solutions for fast and secure internal communication.
WAN: Wide Area Network services for connecting multiple locations.
G-PON Solutions: Gigabit Passive Optical Network services for high-speed, fiber-based connectivity.
Service Provider Core & Corporate Network Implementation: Custom solutions for building and managing core networks.

2. Network Security Next Generation Firewalls: Advanced firewalls that offer more than just basic protection, including threat intelligence and malware detection.
Web Security: Protection against online threats, including phishing, malware, and other web-based attacks.
Email Security: Securing email communication to prevent spam, phishing, and data breaches.
Web Application Firewall: Protecting web applications from cyber threats and vulnerabilities.
ADC: Application Delivery Control: Optimizing the delivery of applications to ensure high availability and performance.
DLP: Data Loss Prevention: Preventing sensitive data from being leaked, stolen, or compromised.
Endpoint Protection: Securing all endpoints, such as computers, mobile devices, and servers, against threats.
Network Visibility and Enforcement: Tools and techniques to provide better visibility into network activity and enforce security policies.
Penetration Testing: Simulated attacks to identify vulnerabilities and strengthen security.
Vulnerability Assessment: Regular assessment to identify and mitigate security risks.
Content Filtering: Blocking harmful or inappropriate content to ensure safe browsing. .

3. Wireless Systems Wireless LAN: Secure and scalable wireless networks for seamless connectivity within facilities.
Point to Point: Direct wireless connections between two locations for efficient data transfer.
Point to Multipoint: Wireless systems that connect multiple locations to a central point, ideal for wide-area coverage.
PTP Bridge: Wireless bridging solutions for connecting separate networks over a distance.
Broadband Wireless: High-speed wireless internet services for continuous connectivity.
Wireless Controller: Centralized management for wireless networks, ensuring smooth and efficient operation.
Wireless Hotspot: Public or private wireless access points for easy internet connectivity.
4. Call Center Solutions
Unified Communication & Collaboration: Streamlining communication across various platforms for better collaboration.
Unified Communications Solutions Include:
Call Management & Control: Efficient management of incoming and outgoing calls.
Voice Mail: Easy-to-manage voice messaging services.
Contact Center Solution: Comprehensive tools to manage customer interactions effectively.
Social Media Collaboration: Integrating social media channels into your communication system.
IP Telephony Solutions: High-quality voice communication over the internet.
Telepresence & Conference Solutions: Solutions for seamless video conferencing and virtual meetings.
Mobile Collaborations: Tools to enhance communication and collaboration on mobile devices.
Cloud Collaboration Solutions: Flexible and scalable cloud-based communication tools.
5. CCTV Systems
Dome Cameras: High-quality dome cameras for effective surveillance in various environments.
External Fixed Cameras: Reliable outdoor cameras designed to withstand harsh conditions.
All-in-One Cameras: Versatile cameras that combine multiple functions in a single device.
Day/Night Dome Cameras with IR LEDs: Cameras that deliver clear footage in both daytime and low-light conditions.
Network "IP" Cameras: Internet Protocol cameras for real-time monitoring and remote access.
Pan Tilt & Zoom Cameras: Cameras with adjustable viewing angles for comprehensive surveillance.
LCD & CRT Monitors: High-resolution monitors for clear and precise viewing of security footage.
Digital Video Recorders (DVR): Systems to record, store, and playback security footage.
Network Video Recorders (NVR): Advanced video recording systems with network integration for easy access.
